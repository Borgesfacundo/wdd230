Visits Information

Population number:

In the city of 9 de Julio we have a total of 48,838 inhabitants.
It is one of the most relevant cities in the northwest of the Province of Buenos Aires due to its geographical location, its agricultural production, its commerce and industry. It is frequently visited by fans of Road Tourism, because there is the Autodromo Ciudad de Nueve de Julio, one of the largest in the highest category of TC in Argentina.

Source: https://www.gba.gob.ar/

9 de Julio's Museum

We have one of the most beautifull museums in all the area, Join us and learn everything about this town here.
The “General Julio de Vedia” Historical Archive and Museum is a state-owned institution and directly managed by the Municipality of 9 de Julio. It was born and developed in the service of the preservation of cultural assets that protect the historical identity of the community of 9 de Julio; and the dissemination of knowledge of these intellectual aspects.

Historical Information

In 1865 the town had 165 homes. On July 19, 1865, the Nueve de Julio party was created by law and by decree of December 20, the first justice of the peace was appointed, installing the court at the beginning of the following year. On February 18, 1866, the first municipal corporation was elected and on January 1, 1886, the first municipality was established, with Dr. Tomas D. West (first doctor of 9 de Julio) as municipal mayor and Alejandro Muzio as president of its Deliberative Council.

9 de Julio's Aeroclub

The aeroclub is a non-profit institution located at the aerodrome of the city of 9 de Julio, province of Buenos Aires, which serves mainly as a flight school, training private airplane and glider pilots. In addition, aircraft hangars, scenic flights, and transfer services are provided. It is a place where anyone with an interest in aviation can go, not only to fly but also to spend a pleasant afternoon in a family and camaraderie atmosphere. In addition to the hangars, belonging to the club and private owners, on the aerodrome grounds, there are grills with tables for rent, a party room, a model airplane barbecue, an aero bar, and a lot of green space with free access for whoever wants to go and spend the day. late. The flying club's flying and instruction activities take place mostly on weekends and holidays. We will wait for you!
