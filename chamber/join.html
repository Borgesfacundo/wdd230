Membership level

NP Membership

This is for non profit organizations and the is no fee. It includes the following benefits: every 6 months you will have an special event focused on your organization, you will have access to 10 trainning sessions per month and 15% discount in special events.

Bronze Membership

This is the basic plan. It includes a 15% discount in every event, an special section on the home page to your spotlights and 12 trainnings sessions per month

Silver Membership

Silver memberships will obtain a 25% discount in every event, 2 sections to special section to advertise your page and access to more than 15 trainning session.

Gold Membership

Gold Membership offers a total of 35% discount in every event, 4 special sections to advertise your business or spotlight whatever you want and unlimited access to our trainning sessions
